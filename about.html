About This Resource

Gynecologic Oncology – Review Questions

This website is an educational resource designed to support the learning and ongoing knowledge development of medical students, residents, fellows, and practicing clinicians in gynecologic oncology.

The questions are structured in an open, viva-style format and are intended to reflect real-world clinical reasoning rather than rote memorization. Each case includes focused sub-questions, model answers, explanations, and references to established clinical frameworks and guidelines.

This platform was created by Yoav Brezinov, MD MSc, gynecologic oncologist, as a practical tool for exam preparation, self-assessment, and teaching.

The content is updated regularly and is designed to complement, not replace, formal guidelines and clinical judgment.
